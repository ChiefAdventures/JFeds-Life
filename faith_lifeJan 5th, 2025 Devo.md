Proverbs 27:17 NLT, “As iron sharpens iron, so a friend sharpens a friend.”

Christian community is so important, and it seems to be more so important as chaos in this world increases. Another verse that pairs nicely with this one also in Proverbs 27 but verse 6 NLT, “Wounds from a sincere friend are better than many kisses from an enemy.” This concept of a friend is one based out of love. Someone who wants what is best for you that only points you towards God. It’ll be tough at times, because your friend may tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear.
