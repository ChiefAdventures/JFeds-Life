Proverbs 27:17

January 5, 2026 • Faith & Life

Proverbs 27:17 NLT, "As iron sharpens iron, so a friend sharpens a friend."

Christian community is so important, and it seems to be more so important as chaos in this world increases. Another verse that pairs nicely with this one also in Proverbs 27 but verse 6 NLT, "Wounds from a sincere friend are better than many kisses from an enemy." This concept of a friend is one based on love. Someone who wants what is best for you that only points you towards God. It'll be tough at times, because your friend may tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear.

One of the best biblical examples of friendship is David and Jonathan. They had each other's backs in every moment, and they weren't afraid to have some disagreements. They even went as far as swearing loyalty to each other in the Lord's name, so that their friendship would never be broken.

I've definitely been someone who looks at friendships in terms of what I can get out of this friendship. If you look at it with the heart to give, to serve then you'll gain something even greater than just surface level friendship. You'll gain someone that's like a brother or sister. Even at points you may disagree or not get along, but you'll have their back no matter what. So that's my challenge for you and myself. If you don't have someone like that, find someone that you're willing to go all in on a friendship with and let it blossom.

  1. What does friendship look like to you?
  2. How can you be a better friend?
  3. How can you speak more in truth to your friends instead of what they might want to hear?

Love you guys, God bless,
