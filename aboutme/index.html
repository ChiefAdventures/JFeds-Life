About Me

Getting to know the person behind the lens

What's up, I'm Jason Feddersen, most people call me JFed. Originally from Nebraska, I'm currently based in North Dakota for school, but I'm looking forward to heading back home soon.

I do what I do because it's where I'm most curious to learn, grow, and willing to fail in order to succeed. Photography, videography, and content creation all keep me exploring new possibilities. Lately, I've been diving deep into AI, learning how to use it from both a business and biblical perspective with Professor McIntosh.

Beyond work, I'm passionate about bowling and ultimate frisbee. I also enjoy mobile gaming and chess...and let's be honest, way too much Taco Bell. It may not be great for my body, but I'll probably eat it until the day I die.

It's been a roller coaster of an adventure so far, and I'm excited to see where God takes me next.
