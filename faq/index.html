Business & Services

I specialize in wedding videography, sports photography, and content creation. I cover everything from intimate ceremonies to large sporting events, delivering high-quality visual content tailored to your needs.

The easiest way to book is through the contact form on my Chief Adventures website. I'll respond within 24-48 hours to discuss availability, packages, and your specific needs. Early booking is recommended, especially for weddings and major events.

Personal & Hobbies

I've been bowling from a young age. I started more casually with my family, and the passion grew for it, because my dad is a bowler. Over time, the passion became my own, and I love talking bowling with anyone that will listen. The challenge, strategy, and mental game of bowling keeps me coming back.

It's very tough to choose. For longevity and the longest passion, I'd have to say bowling. Ultimate frisbee is a close second though, because of the team dynamics and athleticism and strategy required for it. Disc golf for me is a great way to get outdoors and spend time with friends.
